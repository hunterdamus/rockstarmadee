DESTROY LONELY

RAPPER, OPIUM LABEL MEMBER

Bobby Wardel Sandimanie III (born July 30, 2001), known professionally as Destroy Lonely, is an American rapper. He is best known for his mixtape No Stylist (2022) which peaked at number 91 on the Billboard 200. He is also the son of rapper I-20 who was signed to Ludacris' label Disturbing tha Peace.

SUCCES HISTORY

Destroy Lonely started making music in 2016, when his school opened several recording studios. In the same year he posted his first single no face, no case on the SoundCloud platform. At school, he met 2 friends, Texaco Cam and Nezzus, with whom he skipped school to record new songs. Over time Destroy Lonely came up with his sound, which consists of atmospheric sounds and synths. With this sound the artist released 3 projects - Darkhorse, Forever, ILY., Sometimes U Lose. But the main breakthrough for him was the single Bane, which went viral in TikTok, and helped him to break out from underground. In 2020 he released Underworld, Lord, Broken hearts, OVERSEAS. Shortly after releasing Broken hearts he was written to by his Opium manager and close friend Playboi Carti, who praised his music and showed it to Playboi Carti who liked it. After that he was signed to the Opium label and released No Stylist and If Looks Could Kill.
